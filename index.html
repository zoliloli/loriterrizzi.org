Lori Terrizzi is the CEO/Founder and Site Architect of GOALOOP - The Goal Market®, Connecting the World through Goals®. Terrizzi holds an MFA from Columbia University in Film and is a Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Rutgers College. Goaloop is the product of her life-long focus on issues of identity, and ongoing study of the relative value and relationship between words and images, form and content, equity and ability, truth and memory, power and substance, one of us and all of us. The story "Raising Liberty: The Construction of the Statue of Liberty as Reconstruction Was Under Attack" served as a metaphor for the construction of Goaloop and can be read here; it is being made into a movie.
